Simple format — columns: Time, Cue, Position, Tempo

With tracks — add Track and Duration columns. Times are relative to each song's start; tracks are built automatically and added to the setlist. Rows with a blank Track cell inherit the track above them (like merged cells).

Load / resistance column — if you have a Load column instead of a Cue column, Load and Tempo are combined as the main cue text (e.g. "H · Power").
